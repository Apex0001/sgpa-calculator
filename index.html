FAQs:-

Q1. What's the difference between SGPA and CGPA?

A: The SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is the average of the grade points or grades for all subjects in a specific semester. On the other hand, the CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the weighted average of all SGPAs across all semesters, taking their respective credits into account..
